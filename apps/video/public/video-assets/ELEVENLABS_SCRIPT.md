# ElevenLabs Audio Voiceover Script (3 Minutes Presentation)

To complete your video presentation with voiceover audio:
1. Go to [ElevenLabs.io](https://elevenlabs.io)
2. Choose a clear, professional voice (e.g. **Adam**, **Antoni**, or **Rachel**)
3. Generate audio using the timestamps & script below.
4. Download the generated `.mp3` audio file, rename it to `voiceover.mp3`, and place it in `apps/web/public/video-assets/voiceover.mp3`.
5. Run `pnpm video:render` to produce the final MP4 video!
